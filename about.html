About

Who is Mr. TV?

Mr. TV is the persona I've adopted based on a random nickname given to me by my wife. As Mr. TV, I am dedicated to the art of keeping retro-gaming alive and well.

What send-in services do you offer?

I offer a variety of services specific to each console. Beyond basic repair and restoration, there are a number of console-specific modification services available such as re-shelling, RGB enhancements, screen upgrades, and many more!
